Thomas D. Brown

I am a PhD student in the Philosophy department at the University of Liverpool.

My primary research interests are the philosophy of language and metaphysics (particularly meaning and falsity). In my PhD project, I aim to develop a theory of propositions that can account for the ontological asymmetry between truth and falsity.
